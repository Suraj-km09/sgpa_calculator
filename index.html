Welcome to AKTU SGPA Calculator

This tool helps you calculate your Semester Grade Point Average (SGPA) and Yearly Grade Point Average (YGPA) according to AKTU's grading system.

Key Features:

  • Calculate SGPA for both Odd and Even semesters
  • Compute YGPA (Yearly GPA) based on semester results
  • Support for theory subjects, labs, and projects
  • Detailed breakdown of marks, grades, and credit points
  • Export results as PDF for your records

Attention !

  1. ⚠️Carefully select the Credits for each subject to get exact SGPA.
  2. ⚠️Input your internal and external marks carefully.
